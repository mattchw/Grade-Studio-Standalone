About Grade Studio

Grade Studio is a students’ performance analyzing web app tailor-made for teachers at universities in Hong Kong.

There are four features in Grade Studio:

1. Gaussian distribution curve


i. Showing the overall performace of the students
ii. Users can select the areas on the curve to assign grades to students

2. Histogram


i. Showing the frequency distribution of the overall score
ii. Users can manually change the bin size from 1 - 10

3. Weighting chart


i. Showing a pie chart and table based on the weighting set in the weight-setting module
ii. Users can change the weighting on the fly and the data will be updated

4. Student performance table


i. A table showing the score and grade of students
ii. Users can give remark to students
